Venture services: two years in..
Castle celebrates it’s 10 year birthday this month. Since inception, we’ve always had an entrepreneurial focus, using our capabilities as a means to accelerate value in businesses - frequently as partners or co-founders of early stage ventures.
Two years ago we formalized our earlier opportunistic partnership approach into a focused strategy we called venture services. The goal was simple - find opportunities where we could add value to a promising early stage business by accelerating development of their software platform and operating as an investor/partner rather than a dev shop.
Since we started this push, we’ve now made $2 million in structured equity investments (typically a SAFE) in ten companies, whereby the investment was generated in a program whereby we covered our cost with baseline fees and earned our profit in equity.
So what are the results so far?
We invested $2 million in 10 companies
5 of the 10 have revenue
> $1 million
3 of the 10 will have revenue run-rates of $3-$5 million in ‘19
Investment in product makes a difference
The company with the highest revenue growth in this group involved a program whereby we accelerated the transition from a rough prototype to a polished and scalable platform. We mitigated the risk of this transition and delivered what was needed to start ramping customer growth.
The CEO shared results of a recent customer survey with us, and we love seeing validation that the one software platform we created together is the number 1 thing customers like about the business. Investment in quality software drives competitive advantage.
Our services business
The venture services business model is a very long-term oriented strategy, and it’s not easy to bootstrap this investing activity. We’re thankful that we have wonderful enterprise, middle market, and early-stage clients that tap into our very senior talent with the best track record around in a standard professional services engagement. We’re always looking for opportunities where we can add value in this capacity, either as a full outsourced product team or helping alongside an internal team.
Investing in the Charlotte technology ecosystem
Castle operates product acceleration programs from Seattle to London… but we live and work here in Charlotte, and we’re huge fans of this city and the potential of our fellow entrepreneurs here.
Michael Inglesby joined us in late 2018 to lead our ecosystem development efforts. Michael grew up in the Washington DC area and has experience working in early stage communities from Washington DC to San Francisco and understands how our Charlotte community could work.
One of Michael’s first projects was helping Sam Smith and Collective Hustle put together the Seed the South event, which was a great example of how our community can come together and build momentum for Charlotte’s technology companies.

About
Castle Digital Partners is a technology firm that partners with management teams to create proprietary software platforms that unlock or accelerate value creation in businesses.
We tackle the software initiatives that matter most in a business. Since 2009, we’ve been building software businesses, transforming services companies into tech-enabled businesses, and replatforming old systems into modern experiences.
Our venture services business model provides the flexibility to structure creative partnerships from traditional fee-for-service engagements to equity-financed programs and joint-ventures. We seek strong alignment and incentives between your business objectives and our results.
